Philosophy

I'm a firm believer that any good technology, software or system should be there to serve us and to simplify a certain process for us, to make our lives easier, to allow us to be more present, and to remove the administrative and digital tasks that have arisen with the abundance of technology that we now have.

There's no use in having a brand new phone, a brand new laptop, a subscription service or a piece of software, whether for business or personal use, if it's not being implemented or used to its full potential. People are often in the habit of purchasing something without fully understanding it or how to use it.

Technology is really there to enable your life, to make your life simpler. I look for solutions across the spectrum to assist you in your digital life, to allow you to be more present, to make things flow easier and to generally just enjoy your technology with less of a headache.
